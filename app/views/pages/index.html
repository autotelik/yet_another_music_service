LESS is more

We have stripped site interaction right back compared to current social music sites.

Reducing the functionality to bare bones, creating a level playing field, encouraging users to engage more, and reducing ability of spammers and bots to game the system.

roadmap to micropayment

We are building a new hybrid, that takes the best concepts from current sharing sites, but builds in this key feature :

Implementation of direct micro payments for streaming, playlists and purchases. i.e artists get paid instantly, not at the whim of tech giants or their label. All payments will be purely crypto based, supporting tiny amounts, with minuscule fees. We are looking at the Lightning Network as probably the most promising technology in this area, in combination with Bitcoin and Litecoin.

Bitcoin

We are proud to be a non ICO Project - We see no current use case for tokens, our intention is to build on top of the existing Lightning and Bitcoin networks.
Bitcoin is the oldest and most secure blockchain, backed by the world's most powerful supercomputer network. Payments will be strictly BTC and LTC based - to leverage the immutability and security of the network.
